Myself

My name is Ellen, most call me Ellie and I answer to pretty much anything. I am 21 years old and recently graduated from the University of Limerick with a Bachelor of Science in Music, media and performance technology. I work part-time as a sound engineer in Glor theatre in Ennis.

My Family

There are four people in my family. My parents Marina and Fergal and my younger sister Micquela. My mother is from oventry, England and my father is from Tulla Co. Clare. Micquela is two years younger than me and she is in her second year of Business Studies at the University of Limerick.

My Hobbies

My hobbies comsist of a combination of music and sport. I play a number of musical instruments, piano and guitar being my preference. I have classical training in piano with theRoyal Irish Academy of Music. I also play the drums, bass, flute, bag pipes and sing a little. Sports is also a huge part of my life. I played camogie, soccer, rugby and basketball as well as doing karate, taek-won-do, kick boxing and gymnastics.Competing at various levels in each.
